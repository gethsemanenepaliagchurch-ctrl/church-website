Our Pastors

Meet the spiritual leaders of our church

Our church is blessed with dedicated pastoral leadership committed to shepherding God's people with love, wisdom, and spiritual guidance. Our pastors are here to serve, teach, and walk alongside you in your faith journey.

Our Leadership Philosophy

Servant Leadership

Following Christ's example, our pastors lead by serving, putting the needs of the congregation first.

Biblical Teaching

Committed to faithful exposition of God's Word, helping believers grow in knowledge and faith.

Compassionate Care

Providing pastoral care, counseling, and support during life's joys and challenges.
