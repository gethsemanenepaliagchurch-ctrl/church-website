Our Ministries

Discover ways to serve and grow in your faith

Worship Ministry

Leading God's people in passionate worship

Our worship ministry is dedicated to creating an atmosphere where people can encounter God through music and song. We lead worship in our Sunday services and special events.

What We Do:

  • Lead Sunday worship services
  • Organize special worship nights
  • Train and develop worship team members
  • Create an atmosphere for God's presence

Youth Fellowship

Building the next generation of believers

Our youth ministry focuses on helping young people grow in their faith, build meaningful relationships, and discover their purpose in God's kingdom.

What We Do:

  • Saturday evening fellowship gatherings
  • Bible studies and discipleship
  • Fun activities and outings
  • Leadership development programs

Prayer Ministry

Interceding for our church and community

Our prayer ministry is the backbone of all we do. We believe in the power of prayer and maintain a strong prayer culture in our church.

What We Do:

  • Tuesday cottage fellowship prayers
  • Early morning prayer meetings
  • Prayer chains for urgent needs
  • Fasting and prayer programs

Children's Ministry

Nurturing young hearts for Jesus

We believe children are a precious gift from God. Our children's ministry provides a safe, fun, and engaging environment where kids can learn about Jesus.

What We Do:

  • Age-appropriate Sunday school classes
  • Interactive Bible lessons and activities
  • Special children's events and programs
  • Character development and values teaching

Community Outreach

Serving the Nepali community with love

We are committed to serving our community and sharing God's love through practical acts of kindness and compassion.

What We Do:

  • Help newcomers settle in Bangalore
  • Provide support during emergencies
  • Visit the sick and elderly
  • Community welfare programs

Discipleship Ministry

Growing deeper in faith and knowledge

Through our discipleship programs, we help believers grow in their understanding of God's Word and develop a deeper relationship with Jesus.

What We Do:

  • Small group Bible studies
  • New believers' classes
  • Mentorship programs
  • Leadership training courses

Get Involved

We believe everyone has gifts and talents to contribute. Whether you're looking to serve, grow, or connect, there's a place for you in our ministries.
