About This Location

Our main church is conveniently located in the Marathahalli area, easily accessible from various parts of East Bangalore. The church is situated within the SGR Dental College Campus, providing ample parking space and a peaceful environment for worship. This location serves as our primary worship center with multiple weekly services and fellowship gatherings.

About This Location

Our branch church is located in the rapidly growing Sarjapur Road area of Bangalore. Established to serve the Nepali community in South-East Bangalore, this location offers convenient access for those living in and around Sarjapur, HSR Layout, and nearby areas. We conduct Sunday worship services in Nepali, providing a warm and welcoming environment for families and individuals.
